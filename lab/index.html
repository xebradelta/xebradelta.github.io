One figure, every pose

Every figure below is the same rig — identical proportions, line weight, and style. The only thing that changes between them is a short list of joint angles. That's the whole trick: poses are data, not drawings, so they can never drift out of style.

Guided hold — the figure animates from the same rig

Pick a pose and press play. The figure interpolates its joint angles to get there, then runs a timed hold that flips sides for you. Motion stays perfectly on-model because it's the same figure the whole time.

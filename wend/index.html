Wend ∞

How to play

Find each listed word in the grid. Press a letter and drag through neighboring squares (up, down, left, right) to spell the word from first letter to last — the path can bend as much as it likes.

Every open square is used by exactly one word, and together the words fill the whole board. Dark squares are not used. If a placed word boxes you in, use Undo to remove it and try another route.

Unlike the daily version, you can play as many boards as you want — tap ＋ for a new puzzle, or ↺ to restart the current one.

To install on iPhone: open in Safari → Share → Add to Home Screen. Works offline once installed.

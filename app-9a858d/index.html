Start here

This is your own daily tracker. It comes pre-filled with a 90-day plan as an example. Change any of it to fit you.

  • Make it yours. Tap the gear icon (top right) → Edit categories & items to add, rename, reorder or delete anything.
  • Pick your Day 1 & length. In the editor, set the start date and how many days the program runs. Day 1 anchors to the date you choose.
  • Check things off. Tap a row to complete it. Counter items (like pushups or water) use the − / + buttons toward a target.
  • Set how often. Each item can repeat every day, on certain weekdays, a number of times per week, or once a week.
  • See the arc. Journey shows your streak and day grid; Plan shows the whole blueprint.
  • Yours & offline. Everything saves on this device only. Use your browser's Add to Home Screen to install it like an app.

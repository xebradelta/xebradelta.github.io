Enter Ebonhallow

In the haunted Region of Ebonhallow, the dead are not forgotten and the unclaimed are never truly lost.

When grief curdles into power and the forgotten begin to stir, the living must choose whether remembrance is mercy… or a prison.

Dark fantasy❖ haunted cemeteries❖ morally complex monsters❖ love with teeth

The Story Hook

In a city that prefers its dead quiet, missing bodies begin moving through old plague routes toward an abandoned royal cemetery.

Cassian Thorne, a rogue with too many secrets, and Vaela Blackwell, a paladin with no patience for violated graves, uncover a system of falsified records, stolen names, and dead roads reopening beneath the fog.

The dead are not only rising.
They are being claimed.

Meet the Characters

Two hearts sworn to the dead — and the raven who judges them both.

Cassian Thorne

Rogue · thief · lock specialist · reluctant hero

Age / Race
185, high elf
Skills
Infiltration, lockpicking, archery, sleight of hand, reading people, escaping consequences with dramatic flair

Charming, sarcastic, theatrical, guarded, secretly tender, allergic to sincerity
Companion
Cawsanova, his overly opinionated raven

About the Author

“Your pain is not weakness.” — Morgan Johnson

Morgan Johnson writes emotionally charged gothic fantasy set within the Region of Ebonhallow, where grief has teeth, monsters have wounds, and healing is never clean.

Her stories are written for readers who have carried pain too deep to summarize and experiences too meaningful to be reduced to a diagnosis or mistaken for weakness.
